## Corporate Sponsorship Opportunities

pyOpenSci is committed to building diverse community that supports free and open Python tools for processing scientific data. Your sponsorship helps us:

- Expand our peer review program to support more scientific Python packages
- Develop accessible training resources and educational content
- Support scholarships for our training events
- Maintain and grow our community infrastructure

## Become a Sponsor

We're actively seeking corporate sponsors who share our commitment to open science and scientific software quality. Sponsorship opportunities are available at various levels.

To learn more about sponsorship opportunities, benefits, and how your organization can support pyOpenSci, please contact us at [media@pyopensci.org](mailto:media@pyopensci.org).
